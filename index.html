NPTO Ontology

The NPTO is an ontology writtten in OWL that describes each atom of the Periodic Table in terms of its valence electron shells. It then has definitions of all families of atoms in terms of these electron configurations. In this way it has most of the knowledge within the Periodic Table, except that implied by the standard presentation.
